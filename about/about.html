OUR MISSION

To create Social, Impactful, Scalable and Sustainable businesses that will solve problems that matter
To bring the global goals to the forefront and work towards it as a community
To build a community of people who will mentor, help and incubate the sustainable solutions.

OUR

STORY

Technology has fueled the exponential growth of industries. Agents of Social change have rarely harnessed this power. We realised the path to sustainalbe growth lied at the intersection of tech and social good. Hack for Social Good is our intiative to leverage emerging tech and solve for the Global Goals. To piece together an ecosystem for problem seekers and solution providers.
